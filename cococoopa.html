About Coco Coopa

Our Story

Founded in 2010, Coco Coopa has been serving up the most delicious and crispy chicken in town. Our secret recipe has been passed down for generations and is sure to satisfy your taste buds. We use only the freshest ingredients and our chicken are always cooked to perfection.

Our Menu

At Coco Coopa, we offer a variety of flavors to choose from including Fried chicken, Burgers, Sandwiches, Wraps and more. Our Chicken are available in both bone-in and boneless options and we also offer a selection of sides and drinks to complete your meal.

Our Locations

We currently have 10 locations in South India, making it easy for you to enjoy our wings no matter where you are. Our restaurants are open 7 days a week for lunch and dinner, and we also offer delivery and Takeaway services.
